Why trust us with your high-end audio equipment?

High-End and boutique audio units differ significantly from general consumer electronics. They require unique thermal soldering practices, extreme low-distortion testing gear, and specifically selected acoustic parts. In our laboratory, you can leave your fears behind.

Why we are the ultimate choice: We operate a fully certified ESD-safe laboratory, utilize high-precision instrumentation (Audio Precision audio analyzers, calibrated digital oscilloscopes), and use exclusively genuine audiophile-grade components (Nichicon Fine Gold, WIMA film capacitors, matched tubes). The entire process is fully insured, and we can provide video verification of diagnostics and testing.

ESD-Safe Laboratory

Our work benches, floors, and soldering iron arrays are fully static-grounded to prevent damage to sensitive IC components.

Precision Analyzers

We use dedicated Audio Precision hardware analyzers and ultra-low noise generators to map and match signal response.

Genuine Audio Components

We source premium parts directly, stocking Nichicon Fine Gold, WIMA film capacitors, ELNA Silmic II, and matched tube pairs.

Video Proof & Insurance

Your device (worth up to tens of thousands of euros) is fully insured in our custody. We provide diagnostics and test run video archives upon request.
