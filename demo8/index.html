FAQ_TELEMETRY

How long does the repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 1 to 3 business days after the diagnostic quote is confirmed. For complex issues or when special components need to be ordered, it may take up to 5-7 business days. We will inform you of the exact timeframe after diagnostics.
Do repaired devices come with a warranty?
Yes, all repair work and replaced components are covered by a 6-month warranty starting from the date you pick up your device.
Do you offer on-site repairs at my home?
To ensure high-quality and ESD safety, all repairs are performed at our specialized laboratory. If your TV is too large, you can order our courier service to bring it to us and deliver it back to you post-repair.
How much does diagnostics cost if I decide not to proceed with repair?
If you choose not to repair the device after diagnostics, only the diagnostic fee applies (15–30 € depending on complexity). If you proceed with the repair, diagnostics are completely free.
