Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

Last updated: 09.07.2026

Fast-Service OÜ (hereinafter "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we process and safeguard information you share with us when visiting our website.

1. Complete Absence of Cookies (No Cookies)

Our website is engineered with a privacy-first and lightweight structure:

  • We do not use cookies (neither session, analytical, nor marketing cookies).
  • We do not write tracking identifiers or store settings in your browser storage.
  • Since we do not use cookies, we do not show cookie consent banners. There is no legal necessity for them.

2. Personal Data We Collect & Its Purpose

We collect data only when you explicitly choose to contact us using our contact form. This includes:

  • Name: To address you and bind the inquiry to a specific person.
  • Contact Details (Email or Phone): To respond to your inquiry and ask clarification questions.
  • Device Category and Fault Description: To formulate a repair quote and check stock parts.

This data is processed solely to handle your repair request. Data is never used for advertising, nor shared with third parties (except when using our external email service provider to route the form submission directly to info@fastservice.ee without logging details).

3. Data Retention and Security

We use appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security measures to safeguard your personal details against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or disclosure.

Inquiries are stored inside our secure email mailbox for as long as necessary to complete the repair, service the 6-month warranty, or satisfy legal accounting requirements.

4. Your GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you possess the following rights:

  • Right to access personal details we store about you.
  • Right to demand correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to demand deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), provided no legal exceptions apply.
  • Right to object to data processing.

To exercise these rights, please write to us at: info@fastservice.ee.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update our privacy guidelines from time to time. Any amendments will be published directly on this page.
