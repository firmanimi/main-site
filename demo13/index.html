Common Inquiries

Details on our careful restoration process and guarantees.

How long does the restoration take?
Most repairs are gently completed within 1 to 3 business days after the diagnostic quote is confirmed. For complex issues or when special components need to be ordered, it may take up to 5-7 business days. We will inform you of the exact timeframe.
Do repaired devices come with a guarantee?
Yes, all repair work and replaced components are covered by a serene 6-month warranty starting from the date you pick up your device.
Do you offer on-site repairs at my home?
To ensure the highest quality and ESD safety, all repairs are performed at our specialized calm laboratory. If your TV is too large, you can order our courier service to carefully transport it.
What if I choose not to proceed with the repair?
If you choose not to repair the device after our assessment, only the diagnostic fee applies (15–30 €). If you proceed with the repair, the diagnostics are completely complimentary.
